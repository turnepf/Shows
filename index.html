What's New

4/26 — Cleaner show rows. Each show is now one tidy line: title, network, rating. Tap the title to expand and see genre, cast, who recommended it, next season dates, who you're watching with, and notes — only the lines with data show up.
4/21 — My Shows link. When logged in, a quick "My Shows" link appears on the home page just below the title.
4/17 — Quick actions. On the Watching list, "Watched it" moves a show to Recommending and "Season done" moves it to Waiting — no dropdown needed.
4/17 — Genre tags. Shows now display genre info (Drama, Comedy, Thriller, etc.) pulled from TMDB. Toggle visibility with the Genres pill.
4/17 — Share this list. Share a member's list with friends via text, email, or clipboard.
4/16 — What Members Are Watching. The home page now shows the most popular shows across all members. Tap + to add any of them to your own list.
4/15 — New domain! Show Picker Club is now at showpicker.club. The old link still works too.
4/15 — Share shows to other members. See a show on someone else's list that you want to add to yours? Tap the + button next to any show to send it to another member's Up Next list. It carries over the rating, network link, actors, and all the details. If you're logged in, your name auto-fills.
4/15 — TV mode. Browse your lists on the big screen. Open showpicker.club/tv in your Fire Stick, Roku, or smart TV browser. Large text, dark background, and remote-friendly navigation. The app will also suggest switching to TV mode automatically if it detects you're on a big screen.
4/15 — Sort your lists. Use the sort dropdown at the bottom of any list to sort by Rating (default), A–Z, or Date Added.
4/15 — Back to all members. Tap the ◀ arrow next to the member name to get back to the main list.
4/15 — Network links sync across members. When someone finds a good direct link for a show, it automatically copies to matching shows on other members' lists (runs once per day).
4/15 — Watching With field. Track who you're watching a show with.
4/14 — Series Complete icon. Shows that have ended get a 🎬 badge.
4/13 — Suggest a Show. Not logged in? You can still suggest shows to any member via the button at the bottom of any list.
4/13 — Toggle pills. Show or hide Ratings, Networks, Recommended By, Dates, and Notes with the pill buttons at the bottom.
4/13 — Season dates. See when the next season starts (and when it ends) pulled automatically from TMDB.
4/13 — Multi-member support. One app, multiple members — each with their own lists and login codes.
4/13 — PWA support. Add Show Picker Club to your phone's home screen for an app-like experience.
4/12 — Initial launch. Add, edit, move, and archive shows across four lists: Watching, Waiting, Recommending, and Up Next. Auto-enriched with IMDB ratings, actors, and network links.
