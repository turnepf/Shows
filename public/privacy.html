Show Picker Club — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Show Picker Club ("the app") is a private, invitation-only TV-show tracker operated by Patrick Turner. This policy describes what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Name and phone number, provided when an operator adds you as a member.
  • Show data you enter: titles, lists, notes, ratings, recommendations, and similar list-related fields.
  • Session activity: timestamps of logins and page loads, used only for activity metrics within the app.
  • Standard server logs: IP address and request timestamps, kept briefly for security and abuse prevention.

How we use it

  • To authenticate you when you log in (via SMS code).
  • To send you notifications when another member recommends or shares a show with you.
  • To display members and their lists to other invited members within the club.
  • To improve the app (recommendation matching, "what members are watching", etc.).

SMS messaging

By providing your phone number you consent to receive (1) login verification codes and (2) notifications when another member recommends or shares a show with you. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You can reply HELP for help, or contact us using the email below. Opting out stops both login codes and notifications; you can opt back in by replying START.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your phone number or personal information with third parties for marketing. The only third parties that handle your data are infrastructure providers required to operate the app:

  • Cloudflare (hosting and database).
  • Twilio (SMS delivery — phone numbers are sent to Twilio for the purpose of delivering messages).
  • OMDB, TMDB (show metadata enrichment — these calls do not include your phone number or other personal data).

Retention and deletion

Your account and data are retained as long as you remain a member. To delete your account, contact the operator at the email below and your records (member, phone number, sessions, and show entries) will be removed from the database.

Children

This app is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended for their use.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: patrick@patrickturner.net.
